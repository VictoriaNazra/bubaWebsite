About Us

Our Company

Creating special moments since 1994

Büba was founded based on a passion to deliver delicious bubble tea and fresh, roasted Premium coffee. We think that a great bubble tea or a cup of coffee inspires a great conversation.
We want our customers to feel welcomed in our bubble tea rooms and for them to be able to share a quality moment with a significant other.

Our values

Smile, it's contagious

We strive to provide high-quality service and excellent products. Being kind hearted, honest, and friendly are our mottos. We aim to have a positive impact in our community.

Our mission 

Spreding love and cups of joy

We want our customers to feel the love that we put into the making of our treats. We aim to unite people and let them enjoy quality time together in our beautiful bubble tea rooms.
